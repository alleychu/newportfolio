Building Management System

The journey of digital transformation is never easy. Thus implementing digital twin products in business takes a bit of a chance. In 2021, we got a precious opportunity to work with the biggest Insurance company in Taiwan as their pioneer project adopting a Building Management System (BMS) for operation management. We built a platform with functions including facility information, alarm reporting, CCTV alarming, facility location mapping, as well as indoor environment and energy monitoring. Partner with a hardware company, there were a lot of valuable insights and lessons learned during the IT & OT integration process. I’m grateful for having the experience of leading this project and building the platform from concept to launch.

Time/ 2021-2022
Location/ Taipei, Taiwan
Use cases/ Operation management for a group of 4 office buildings
Role/ Product Manager
